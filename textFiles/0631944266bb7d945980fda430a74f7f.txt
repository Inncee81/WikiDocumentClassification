Google Mashup Editor is an online mashup creation service created by Google.
On January 15, 2009 Vic Gundotra, Google's VP of Engineering announced that the Mashup Editor would be migrated to the Google App Engine, stating "Existing Mashup Editor applications will stop receiving traffic in six months, and we hope you will join our team in making the exciting transition to App Engine."
