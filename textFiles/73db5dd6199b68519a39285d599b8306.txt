This is a list of online spreadsheets. Online spreadsheets are online applications that allow users to create a spreadsheet, have multiple persons edit it and share it with the world.

[edit] Free Software

Simple Spreadsheet - Free and open source
wikiCalc - Free, download application for online use


[edit] Proprietary Software

BadBlue - a small footprint websever able to serve Excel spreadsheets
EditGrid - Free personal accounts. Premium account for organizations.
Expresso spreadsheet - Free, Workgroup, & Enterprise accounts
Google Docs - Free
ThinkFree Calc - Free and Premium services
ZCubes - Calci - Free and no download needed
Zoho Office Suite - Free and Pro accounts
Resolver One - Free
