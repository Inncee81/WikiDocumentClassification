Dijkstra is a Dutch family name that may refer to:

Edsger W. Dijkstra (1930–2002), computing scientist
Rineke Dijkstra (born 1960), photographer
Sjoukje Dijkstra (born 1942), retired figure skater
Bram Dijkstra, professor of English literature
Sieb Dijkstra (born 1966), goalkeeping coach and former goalkeeper
Wieke Dijkstra (born 1984), Dutch field hockey player


Dijkstra's algorithm, conceived by Edsger Dijkstra is a graph search algorithm that solves the single-source shortest path problem
