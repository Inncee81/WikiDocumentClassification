[edit] Places

Principality of Sealand, a disputed micronation on an offshore facility off the coast of Suffolk
Sealand, Flintshire in the River Dee estuary near Hawarden, Wales
A translation of and non-standard spelling of Zealand (Sjælland), a Danish island
A translation of and non-standard spelling of Zeeland, also "Zealand", a Dutch province and village
RAF Sealand, Royal Air Force station in Flintshire, north Wales
Ranrike's Saevo mountain range, translated as "sea land"
