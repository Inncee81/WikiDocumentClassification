This article is about the district in Switzerland.  For the method in human-computer interaction, see GOMS.
Goms is a district of the canton of Valais in Switzerland.
It comprises the following municipalities:

Bellwald
Binn
Blitzingen
Ernen
Fiesch
Fieschertal
Grafschaft
Lax
Münster-Geschinen
Niederwald
Obergoms
Reckingen-Gluringen
