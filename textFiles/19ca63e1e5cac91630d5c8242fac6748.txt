New World Order may refer to:

General


New world order, period of history evidencing a dramatic change in world political thought and the balance of power
New World Order (conspiracy theory), conspiracy theory in which a group is conspiring to rule the world via world government
Novus Ordo Seclorum, motto on the reverse of the Great Seal of the United States often mistranslated as "New World Order," but actually Latin for "New Order of the Ages"


Books


The New World Order, 1940 book by H. G. Wells
The New World Order, 1944 book by Maulana Muhammad Ali
The New World Order, 1990 book by A. Ralph Epperson
The New World Order, 1991 book by Pat Robertson
The New World Order, 2004 science fiction novel by Ben Jeapes


Entertainment


New World Order (professional wrestling), stable in World Championship Wrestling (WCW) and World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE)
New World Order, online team-based tactical first-person shooter video game
"N.W.O.", 1992 song by the industrial band Ministry
The New World Order, 1996 album by the hip-hop group Poor Righteous Teachers
New World Order, 1997 album by Curtis Mayfield
Illuminati: New World Order, 1995 collectible card game
The New World Order, dramatic sketch by Harold Pinter
New World Order (Mage: The Ascension), fictional organisation in the White Wolf role-playing game Mage: The Ascension
"New World Order", track 2 of the album Animatronic by The Kovenant
"New World Order", track 5 of the album No World Order by German power metal band Gamma Ray

Earliest recorded use

New world order (Bahá'í), Bahá'ís believe that it embodies God's divinely appointed plan for the unification of mankind in this age. The term was first coined by their Prophet in His work The Most Holy Book (1873).
