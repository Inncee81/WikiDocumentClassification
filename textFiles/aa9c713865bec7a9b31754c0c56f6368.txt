The term Tai Chi can refer to:

Taiji (in pinyin format) or T'ai-chi (Wade-Giles transliteration), a concept in Chinese philosophy
Tai chi chuan, a Chinese martial art often shortened to "Tai Chi" or "Taiji" in everyday use
Tai chi chih, a westernized, standardized version of Tai Chi
Taichi Kamiya, a character in Digimon
Hong Taiji (Hung T'ai-chi), an early Qing dynasty Manchu emperor
Taiji, the era name of Emperor Ruizong of Tang
Tai Chi (TVB), the television series broadcast by TVB
Tai Chi (Band) a pop band from Hong Kong
